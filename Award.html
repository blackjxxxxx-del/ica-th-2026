Conference Awards

ICA-TH 2026 recognises outstanding research contributions from presenters across all career stages and submission categories.

Two Awards at ICA-TH 2026

Two awards will be presented at the closing ceremony on 7 June 2026, recognising excellence in research presentation and scholarly communication.

🏆

Best Oral Presentation Award

Presented to the individual or panel that demonstrates the highest quality of research design, argument, methodological rigour, and scholarly communication in an oral presentation format.

🥇

Best Poster Presentation Award

Presented to the presenter whose poster demonstrates the strongest research contribution, visual communication, and engagement with the conference theme and tracks.

What Winners Receive

Award winners are recognised at the closing ceremony and receive ongoing benefits beyond the conference itself.

📜

Official Certificate

A signed certificate of award recognition issued by the ICA-TH 2026 organising committee and co-hosting institutions.

🌐

Website Recognition

Winners are featured prominently on the official ICA-TH 2026 conference website with their name, institution, and paper title.

📰

Expedited AJPOR Review

Award winners are invited to submit their full paper to the Asian Journal for Public Opinion Research (AJPOR) for expedited editorial consideration.

Judging Process

Awards are evaluated by an independent panel of senior scholars using transparent criteria aligned with ICA standards.

01

Independent Evaluation Panel

Awards are assessed by an independent panel of three or more senior communication scholars with no conflict of interest with nominees.

02

Evaluation Criteria

Presentations are assessed on research quality, methodological rigour, relevance to the conference theme, clarity of argument, and scholarly contribution to the field.

03

Conflict of Interest Protocol

All panel members declare conflicts of interest prior to evaluation. Panellists recuse themselves from judging presentations where a conflict exists.

04

Announcement at Closing Ceremony

Winners are announced during the closing ceremony on 7 June 2026. All presenters are considered eligible unless otherwise specified.

👨‍⚖️

The Award Panel

Three or more senior communication scholars, drawn from ICA member institutions across the region, evaluate all eligible presentations independently.

Panel members are announced prior to the conference. Nominations are not required — all eligible presenters are automatically considered.

Winners Announced at Closing Ceremony

Both awards will be presented live at the closing session of ICA-TH 2026, bringing together all in-person and virtual delegates to celebrate outstanding research.

📅   7 June 2026 — Bangkok & Online
