About the Conference

The ICA Regional Hub Thailand 2026 is an official concurrent event of the 76th Annual Conference of the International Communication Association.

Hosted at Chulalongkorn University in Bangkok on 6–7 June 2026, the Hub brings together 200 scholars, practitioners, and policymakers to examine communication inequalities through a Southeast Asian lens.
