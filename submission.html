Call for Abstracts

ICA-TH 2026 invites scholars, researchers, and practitioners to submit original research addressing communication inequalities and emerging media dynamics.

Submission Overview

The ICA Regional Hub Thailand 2026 welcomes abstract submissions from communication scholars and interdisciplinary researchers. Selected contributions will participate in the hybrid programme and may be featured in the Global South Plenary.

Conference Tracks

Digital Divides & Algorithmic Justice

Research on digital inequality, algorithmic bias, AI governance, and platform labour across Asian societies.

01

Representation & Marginality

Studies of media representation, migrant narratives, minority voices, and cultural identity.

02

Health & Environmental Communication

Communication research on public health messaging, climate narratives, and environmental justice.

03

Language & Decolonial Knowledge

Exploring linguistic power structures in academia and decolonising communication research.

04

Disinformation & Civic Participation

Research on misinformation ecosystems, polarisation, and democratic engagement.

05

AI Governance & Innovation

Policy frameworks, media automation, and ethical AI communication practices.

06

Marketing Communication & Inclusion

Sustainability communication, CSR messaging, and inclusive digital marketing practices.

07

Submission Guidelines

Abstract length: 250–300 words
Submissions must present original research
Authors should clearly identify research questions, methods, and expected contributions
All abstracts must be submitted in English
Each author may submit a maximum of two abstracts
Accepted presenters must register for the conference
