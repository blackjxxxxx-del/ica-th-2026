Venue & Travel Information

ICA-TH 2026 will take place at the Faculty of Communication Arts, Chulalongkorn University, located in the heart of Bangkok.

Conference Venue

Faculty of Communication Arts

The ICA Regional Hub Thailand 2026 will be hosted at Chulalongkorn University, Thailand's oldest and most prestigious academic institution.

Located in the heart of Bangkok, the Faculty of Communication Arts provides a high-tech environment with modern multimedia auditoriums and dedicated networking spaces for international scholars.

Getting to Bangkok

01
✈️

International Hub

Arrive at Suvarnabhumi Airport (BKK), the primary gateway with world-class facilities, located about 40 minutes from the city center.

02
🚆

Seamless Connection

Take the Airport Rail Link to Phaya Thai Station, or use the MRT Blue Line to Sam Yan Station for direct access to the university.

03
📍

Final Destination

The Faculty of Communication Arts is just a short walk from MRT Sam Yan. Taxis and Grab are also available 24/7 for your convenience.
